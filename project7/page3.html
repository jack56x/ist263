Workout Plan

Push Day (Day 1)

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
  • Bench Press: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Overhead Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Flyes: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Cool-down: Stretching exercises for chest, shoulders, and triceps

Pull Day (Day 2)

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
  • Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Cool-down: Stretching exercises for back and biceps

Leg Day (Day 3)

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
  • Squats: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets x 10-12 reps (each leg)
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Cool-down: Stretching exercises for legs and lower back

Notes

  • Start with a weight that challenges you but allows you to complete the recommended reps with good form. As you progress, gradually increase the weight.
  • Rest for 60-90 seconds between sets and exercises.
  • Ensure you maintain proper form throughout each exercise to prevent injury.
  • Aim to perform this workout plan 3 times a week, with a rest day in between each workout day.
  • Stay hydrated during your workouts, and consider incorporating core exercises and cardiovascular workouts on your rest days for a well-rounded fitness routine.
